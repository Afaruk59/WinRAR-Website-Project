Data Protection Provisions for our Newsletter:

You can subscribe to our free newsletter regarding WinRAR and RAR software by giving us your explicit consent. Solely your email address is mandatory for the subscription. All additional details (surname, name, salutation, language, company, country) are only used to personalize the newsletter and to provide the newsletter in your preferred language.

We use a Double-opt-in-process for the subscription. In the first step we will send an email to the provided email address and ask for your confirmation of the subscription.

In the case that you do not confirm your subscription, we will delete your request automatically within 48 hours. After your confirmation we will process your email address and the additional details (contact data) to personalize and dispatch the newsletter. For the dispatching of the newsletter, we will transmit your email address [and the additional details] to the following service provider:

CleverReach GmbH & Co. KG, Muehlenstr. 43, 26180 Rastede, Germany

The legal basis for the processing is Art. 6 (1) a) General Data Protection Regulation.

You can withdraw your consent to receive the newsletter at any time. To unsubscribe from our newsletter, you can use the link provided in the newsletter or use this link. After your cancelation we will delete your contact data from our database.

We use Google's reCaptcha service to determine whether a human or a computer is making a particular entry in our contact form or newsletter form. Google uses the following data to check whether you are a human or a computer: IP address of the terminal device used, the website you visit with us on which the captcha is embedded, the date and duration of the visit, the recognition data of the browser and operating system type used, Google account if you are logged in to Google, mouse movements on the reCaptcha areas and tasks that require you to identify images. The legal basis for the described data processing is Art. 6 (1) lit. f of the General Data Protection Regulation. There is a legitimate interest on our side in this data processing to ensure the security of our website and to protect us from automated entries (attacks).
