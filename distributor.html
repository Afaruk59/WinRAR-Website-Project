Become A Distributor

We at win.rar GmbH manage the worldwide distribution of RAR software.

Over the past years we have built a network of distribution partners worldwide to support our customers locally.

We are still looking for qualified distributors in many countries worldwide to cooperate with.

  • Do you have experience in the software market?
  • Can you dedicate time and energy to WinRAR?
  • Can you help us expand our PR and marketing activities in your country?
  • Do you have sales experience with corporate customers and through resellers?
  • Do you have qualified personnel/resellers to support your customers efficiently?
  • Are you looking for an outstanding product to increase your revenues?

As an experienced software distributor you know your home market and you know the importance of offering your customers leading software titles.

We are offering you the chance to distribute WinRAR in your market and be a part of a tremendous success story.

When you qualify to become one of our WinRAR distribution partners we will support you in getting started and assist you with all questions you may have about selling WinRAR.

You will in turn support our customers locally and significantly increase WinRAR's market share in your home market.

Be a part of our official WinRAR distributor network. You will partner with one of the industry leaders and generate high turnover in a growing market.

How does it work?

  1. Please send us your company presentation via email (only English applications can be considered)
    Include:
    • Country you operate in
    • Complete contact data
    • Company Benchmark Data
    • Customer profile
    • Product profile
    • Customer service offered
    • Ideas on how you plan to sell WinRAR

    Send your email to partners(at)win-rar.com

  2. We will contact you personally after reviewing your application to discuss further steps.

More detailed information about being a WinRAR distributor:

  • No need to purchase a minimum amount of licenses within a certain period.
  • No physical delivery of licenses necessary; licenses are delivered electronically by email. This means there won't be any extra costs for shipping.
  • You will order your customers' registration keys using our special WinRAR ordering system.
  • Registration keys arrive within 48 hours at the very most.
    Normally you will receive your customers registration keys within a few hours.
  • Payment:
    You will receive a monthly invoice listing all orders and the total amount due. Payment is accepted via international money transfer.

We are looking forward to your application (partners(at)win-rar.com).
